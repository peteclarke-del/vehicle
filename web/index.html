Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

A complete vehicle management toolkit that grows with your garage.

Vehicle Profiles

Complete profiles with make, model, year, specs, images, depreciation tracking, and registration lookup. Every detail at a glance.

Fuel Tracking

Log every fill-up. See MPG, cost per mile, monthly trends, and spending breakdowns. Snap receipt photos instantly.

Service History

Detailed service records with line items, labour costs, parts used, and provider info. Link services to MOT work.

MOT Records

Track test results, advisories, failures, and expiry dates. Import full DVSA history with one click. Never miss a renewal.

Insurance & Tax

Manage policies, premiums, cover levels, and renewal dates. Track road tax status with automatic expiry notifications.

Parts & Consumables

Inventory management for parts, fluids, filters. Track suppliers, warranties, replacement intervals, and costs.

Smart Notifications

Real-time alerts for upcoming MOTs, expiring insurance, tax renewals, and overdue services. Stay ahead, never behind.

Custom Reports

Generate PDF and Excel reports. Fuel analysis, cost breakdowns, vehicle overviews â€” from built-in templates or your own.
